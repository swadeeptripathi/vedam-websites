Our Story

Where Dhaba Meets Fine Dining

Shan E Rewa is more than a restaurant — it's the pride of Rewa. Born from a deep love for authentic North Indian flavours and the rich culinary heritage of the Vindhya region, we bring together the warmth of a traditional dhaba with the elegance of fine dining.

Every dish is prepared with hand-ground spices, time-honoured recipes passed down through generations, and the freshest local ingredients. From sizzling tandoori platters to aromatic biryanis, each meal is a celebration of Rewa's royal food culture.

Whether you're here for a family feast, a romantic dinner, or a quick meal with friends — Shan E Rewa welcomes you home.
