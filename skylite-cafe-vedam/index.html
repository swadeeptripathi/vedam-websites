About Skylite

WHERE THE SKY IS YOUR CEILING

Perched above the city of Satna, Skylite Cafe Restaurant Rooftop is where twilight skies meet culinary brilliance. Our open-air setting, accented with warm lights and a panoramic view, creates the perfect backdrop for every occasion.

From romantic date nights to vibrant group celebrations, from weekend brunches to weeknight escapes — Skylite offers an experience that goes beyond dining. Our menu spans from desi classics to continental favorites, each crafted to delight.

Step up, look out, and dine above the ordinary.
