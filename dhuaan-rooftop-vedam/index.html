Our Story

WHERE SMOKE MEETS SOPHISTICATION

Perched atop the skyline of Rewa, Dhuaan Rooftop Restaurant is more than a dining destination — it's an experience. The name "Dhuaan" (smoke) captures our essence: bold flavors kissed by flame, served under open skies with the city lights below.

Our chefs blend traditional North Indian recipes with global influences, crafting dishes that tell stories of spice, fire, and passion. From sizzling kebabs to aromatic biryanis, every plate is a celebration.

Whether you're here for a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a private celebration, Dhuaan promises an evening you won't forget.
