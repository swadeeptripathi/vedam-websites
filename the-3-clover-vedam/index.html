About The 3 Clover

Where elegance meets flavor

Nestled in the heart of Rewa at Shanti Palace Square, The 3 Clover is a premium dining destination that brings together the grandeur of buffet dining and the finesse of à la carte cuisine.

Our carefully curated menus feature the finest North Indian, Chinese, and Continental dishes, prepared by experienced chefs who believe in the magic of fresh ingredients and authentic recipes.

Whether it's a weekday dinner, a weekend family feast, or a celebration to remember, The 3 Clover transforms every meal into a special occasion.
