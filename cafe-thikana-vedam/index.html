The Thikana Concept

More than a cafe — it's your place

"Thikana" means a place where you belong. Cafe Thikana 2.0 was born from a simple idea: Rewa deserves a cafe that feels like home, serves food that surprises, and creates memories that last.

With natural wood interiors, hanging greenery, and sunlit corners, every visit feels like a mini getaway. Our kitchen serves everything from desi tandoori favorites to international dishes — all made with fresh ingredients and love.
